Symbolic rule packet.
A code has one shape symbol and one mark word.
The shape chooses the material:
- ◇ means copper.
- ▲ means glass.
- ◆ means stone.
- ✚ means linen.
The mark chooses the state:
- zor means sealed.
- vek means open.
- lum means flagged.
- pav means archived.
To decode a code, combine material then state.
Examples: ◇ zor means copper sealed. ▲ vek means glass open.
