Neral-1 is an invented language.
Sentence order is: TENSE VERB SUBJECT OBJECT.
Adjectives follow nouns in Neral but precede nouns in English.

Tense words: na=present, pa=past.
Nouns: mip=lantern, tav=harbor, lud=scribe, bem=orchard, rok=copper, zel=mirror.
Verbs: nosh=carries/carried, kiv=guards/guarded, daru=mends/mended, vex=weighs/weighed.
Adjectives: su=silver, gar=broken, no=quiet, fim=narrow.

Examples:
- na nosh lud mip -> the teacher sees the cat
- pa kiv tav bem -> the dog liked the bird
- na daru rok su zel gar -> the small child helps the big artist
